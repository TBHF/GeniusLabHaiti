The best way to prepare for our demanding Genius Lab Haiti™ leadership training program is to start thinking about your areas of passion and identify the business and tech skills you'll need to make an impact.

Thankfully, there are a number of excellent free online resources worth the time. Here is a list of some of the ones we use all the time:

  • Devenir entrepreneur du changement by HEC Paris
  • What is Web Literacy by the Mozilla Learning Network
  • Making Something People Love by Google Tools for Entrepreneurs
  • How to Create a Basic Website by Knightlab at Northwestern University
  • Make Your First Data Product by Google Data Studio
  • Live Long and Prosper: A Lecture by Steve Blank at New York University (2011)
